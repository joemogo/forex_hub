Automatic top-down scan
When ON, every pair's Weekly/Daily/4H bias is derived mechanically from live OANDA candles (EMA trend + swing structure) once an hour, and Bucket/Grade are set automatically: 2/3+ agreement + a real weekly/daily AOI = Active Watch, which feeds straight into Auto Trading. Turn this off any time to go back to setting Weekly/Daily/4H yourself in the table below.
Automatic top-down scan is OFF — Weekly/Daily/4H fields stay as you set them.

Auto Paper Trading
When ON, every scan cycle (~60s) checks every Active Watch pair against your full rule set, and opens a paper trade the moment everything lines up — no clicking needed. Conditions checked, all required:
  • Pair is on your Active Watch list (set via Sunday Scan — top-down bias already agreed)
  • Session is active (London or New York — no off-hours entries)
  • Live confluence score ≥ 65% in the signal's direction
  • A fresh engulfing candle trigger matching that direction and bias just fired
  • A valid AOI (support for buys / resistance for sells) exists to set the stop
  • Resulting trade is at least 1:2 R:R
  • No trade already open on that pair, and it hasn't already auto-traded that pair today
This always checks its own dedicated 15M candles for the entry trigger, no matter what timeframe you have selected on the Scanner chart — feel free to browse any timeframe while it's running.
