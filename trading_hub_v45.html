By rule variant — current 2-candle standard vs legacy 5-candle rule
Compares trades logged under the new standard 2-candle engulf rule (adopted 30 Jun) against historical trades logged under the old 5+ candle swallow rule. The 5-candle threshold had no basis in published price-action practice and was likely costing trade frequency without a proven accuracy benefit — this comparison will show whether that holds true going forward. Needs roughly 20-30 trades per group before the comparison is meaningful.
By signal timeframe — 15M-engulf (original) vs 1H-engulf (test)
One-variable-at-a-time test: watching the entry signal on 1H against the same Weekly/Daily AOI/bias, instead of waiting for the 15M engulf. Not yet decided which is standard — needs enough trades in both groups before drawing conclusions.
H&S / Inverse H&S confluence — log-only, not yet a grading factor
Tracks whether a Head & Shoulders or Inverse H&S pattern (confirmed only once the neckline closes through) was present at the AOI. This does NOT affect grade, position sizing, or trade selection — logged only to check later whether H&S-confirmed AOIs show better expectancy than AOIs without it, once enough trades accumulate.
